Discover The Valley

Utah Welcome Sign

Here is the Welcome to Utah Sign! Without this great state the Valley's of West and South Jordan would not exsist. Back when Utah was first founded it was nothing more than a dry desert. Today it is far from a desolate desert, it is now a thriving green, and growing ecosystem for both people and animals. This sign helps remind people who come to Utah that their dreams are obtainable, and that their buisness will flourish!

The Great Salt Lake

Here are two pictures of the Great Salt Lake, a staple for the state of Utah. The Great Salt Lake is where tons of minerals from the rivers gets collected and contained in one spot. Thus making it salty. The Jordan Valley's are a part of Salt Lake City. Another cool fact, the Jordan River is one of the rivers that flows into the Great Salt Lake. To us it represents the relationship and importance the Jordan Valley has to Salt Lake City, providing "minerals" so to speak just as the river has to the lake to give the city it's unique, "flavor".

The Capital Building

Here is another amazing attraction to the great City of Salt Lake, the State Capital Building. Here is where laws are made to help provide support and aid to local buisnesses in the Jordan Valley, and all over Utah. This is a super valuable and special spot for us.

Many Mountains

Here is a picture of the many mountains you can find here in the Jordan Valley, one of which is the Wasatch Mountains. There are many different mountains that you can hike on, and spend time with your family here in the Jordan Valley.
